You sunburnt sicklemen, of August weary,

Come hither from the furrow, and be merry:

Make holiday,; your rye-straw hats put on,

And these fresh nymphs encounter every one

In country footing.

Enter certain Reapers,, properly habited: they join with the Nymphs in a graceful dance; towards the end whereof Prospero starts 59 suddenly, and speaks; after which, to a strange, hollow, and confused noise, they heavily vanish. Pros. [_Aside_] I had forgot that foul conspiracy

Of the beast Caliban and his confederates

Against my life: the minute of their plot

Is almost come. [_To the Spirits._] Well done! avoid; no more!
